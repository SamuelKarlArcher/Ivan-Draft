What order should one place different values in life?

The will to live.

This is so fundamental to life that we often fail to see how important it is. It is the very life force within us that makes us want to go on with life despite the many struggles. Many religions and spiritualities view this creative force as GOD, most of us in times of crisis look to our faith in our beliefs to go on. It is a selfish desire and puts ones survival above all others, hence the modern term, Love of self. Fundamentalist extreme religious belief or a dictator can result in a perfectly healthy individual voluntarily taking their life. In times of crisis such as war, others are asked to risk their lives for a cause; many have fallen for their country! Our conscious intellect is our most advanced or weakness for survival, depending on the rationality of our thinking and observation of the reality of life. Put simply, humans have the capacity to choose to live. It has also led to modern phrases like, Love of life or Compassion for life.

The basic essentials of survival.

This is the water, food and shelter from bad weather conditions that each individual requires to stay alive. Almost half of the world population lives on US $2.5 per day. Approximately R500/month.

Health of the individual.

After famine, wars and natural disasters, it is 4. disease which is our biggest problem with living our lives. Mental health is probably more important than physical. Serious physical disability can be overcome if the mind is healthy.

Work.

This is ones main occupation that makes up the days of our lives. Work may be as a house executive, collecting wood and water or running a company. It usually is required to be able to provide for 2.

Relationships.

Often when patients are asked, “what is their main priority in life?” this is the answer. There are relationships such as “Romeo and Juliet”, a Shakespearian tragedy in which a love is of such intensity that it may be the will to live. In most relationships it is necessary to take care of 1 to 4 first to ensure you are of value to the relationship.

Benevolence, Compassion or Reverence for life.

Ones ability to associate with others and have an understanding of the common life force in all of us. This is an appreciation of the co dependence of ecological systems and diversity of life on our planet. Also the rarity of life in the known universe.

Others such as recreation, art and creativity.

The order and type of activity can vary according to the individual.
