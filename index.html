About Us

For over three decades, Dr. L.M Archer "Riverdoc" has been providing compassionate and comprehensive healthcare services in Colenso, South Africa. As a well-established general practitioner, The Practice offers a host of medical services, including a pharmacy, travel clinic, emergency care, as well as occupational medical care. With a passion for holistic community care, Dr. Archer "Riverdoc" strives to give his patients a one-stop medical destination, minimizing unnecessary travel.

The practice specializes in ultrasound, dispensing, and emergency medicine, while Dr. Archer "Riverdoc" also conducts research to better understand complex issues affecting his patients.
